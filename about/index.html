About Win.Win

Eric Wannon – Upholding Justice, Delivering Excellence

With 35 years of legal and business experience, I specialize in mediation, blending fairness with practical solutions. Starting in litigation before founding EAW Advisors, my career spans various industries, shaping my approach to conflict resolution.

My background in complex litigation, handling disputes worth over $500 million, honed my negotiation skills. This experience laid the groundwork for my effective approach in mediation, focusing on fair outcomes.

Leading companies in international trade, technology, and real estate broadened my perspective, enriching my mediation approach to address conflicts with diverse insights.
